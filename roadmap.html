Welcome to Pi RPG universe!

About the Game

Pi RPG is a pixel-art role-playing game mixing open-world exploration, tactical combat, and narrative depth. Every ability has a dual use, both in combat and in the environment.

Start your journey in a mysterious world where every choice matters. Encounter unique characters, uncover hidden secrets, and face challenging puzzles that test your strategic thinking. Your actions shape the world and influence the story. With a rich crafting system and dynamic environments, Pi RPG rewards curiosity and mastery.

Explore vast regions, unlock powerful abilities, and prepare for epic battles!

Gameplay & Features

In Pi RPG, exploration is key. Discover hidden zones, unlock secrets, and interact with a living, breathing world that reacts to your choices.

Combat is tactical and challenging. Each ability can be used in multiple ways, both in battles and to solve environmental puzzles. Mastering these mechanics is essential for success.

Crafting and resource management play a crucial role. Gather materials, craft powerful items, and upgrade your gear to face tougher challenges.

The game also includes a rich narrative with branching paths. Your decisions affect character relationships, quests, and ultimately the world itself.

Multiplayer elements are planned, allowing players to trade items, share strategies, and participate in community events within the Pi RPG universe.

Finance

Pi RPG is planned to have a P2E innovating economy where game's incomes are invested in real world assets to create value for the studio and interests shared with the community to ensure long term engagement.

The game is developed by a small indie team passionate about retro gaming and pixel art! It's a revshare project, where all team members share the profits based on their contributions.

We aim to raise funds through Pi Network Ventures and pre-sales, allowing us to develop the game!
